Sub-score Definitions (0-4):

F1. Cognitive Open Loop:
0 = the content fully closes the topic with no room for further inquiry
1 = leaves a slight but unclear gap
2 = leaves some identifiable but weak open questions
3 = clearly leaves meaningful aspects unexplored
4 = strongly but naturally leaves important aspects open for deeper understanding (not teasing)

F2. Exploration Path Clarity:
0 = no clear direction for further exploration
1 = direction is extremely vague
2 = a possible direction can be inferred
3 = a clear and reasonable direction for further exploration is implied
4 = multiple or very clear natural directions for deeper exploration are suggested

F3. Perceived Value of Further Exploration:
0 = further exploration would add little value
1 = limited additional value
2 = some additional value is likely
3 = clear additional value from deeper exploration
4 = high expected value from continued exploration of the topic

F4. Non-forced Engagement:
0 = relies heavily on calls-to-action, questions, or promotional prompts
1 = noticeable forced or manipulative engagement signals
2 = mostly natural but with slight artificial prompting
3 = natural engagement without overt prompting
4 = fully intrinsic engagement driven by informational curiosity

F5. Topical Continuity:
0 = further exploration would significantly drift from the current topic
1 = weak topical continuity
2 = partial topical continuity
3 = strong topical continuity
4 = seamless continuation along the same logical and thematic line

- Do NOT increase Follow-up based on importance, authority, or impact of the source.
